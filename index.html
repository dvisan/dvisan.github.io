About

Graduated in 2016 with a degree in Medical Engineering from the Polytechnic University of Bucharest and soon after, I started working in the medical and clinical software industry on consultancy and QA positions.

Having a technical healthcare background along an innate passion for technology and a constant drive for innovation makes it easier to keep up with the industry, to both identify and understand problems more thoroughly, to ask better questions and nonetheless, to provide the most adequate solutions every time. Therefore, I'm really into everything about e-Health.

I'm self-taught in the technology area, mostly learning stuff out of the pure curiosity to find out how certain technologies, services, software packages or websites work. I'm not proficient in a certain technology, but I'm able to understand how they work and where to look for information when the big picture is not complete. Due to the increasing volume of information available, it all comes down to asking better questions.
